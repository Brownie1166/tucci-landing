Exclusive experience

A day at
Michele's farm

Spend a full day at Fattoria Cuscusa — the organic farm and dairy that Stanley Tucci visited in Sardinia. Michele Cuscusa, third-generation shepherd and organic pioneer, will take you through cheese making, wine tasting from his own vines, and lunch cooked together at his table with his family.

This is not a recreation of what you watched. It is the real thing — the same farm, the same hands, the same table. Available as a standalone day experience or as part of the 12-day harvest tour.
